This page will cover non-main characters specific to Season 3 Episode 7, Son of Coma Guy.

This is detective Michael Tritter. One day, Tritter goes to a clinic to get his "special stick" checked. He waits 2 hours at the clinic, and once he eventually does get into the check-up room, House treats him like shit. While house is about to leave the clinic room, Tritter even ends up kicking his cane causing House to trip. House then angrily uses an anal thermometer on tritter, and before taking it out, house just leaves the room, leaving tritter there alone for hours with the thermometer in his ass. Turns out, Tritter was actually a police officer, so now he's trying to bust House's drug addiction as revenge! (his real motives are that he wants to give house a hard time so he'll learn to be nicer to people)

In the previous episode, Tritter questions Wilson about House's drug addiction. Wilson learns that House actually stole his prescription pad, and prescribed drugs for himself. Allowing him to gain access to more drugs than ever before, but also endangering Wilson's job.
